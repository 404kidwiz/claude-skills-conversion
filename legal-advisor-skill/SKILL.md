This skill provides comprehensive legal advisory services for technology and business matters. It delivers expert guidance on contract management, compliance frameworks, intellectual property protection, and risk mitigation strategies while enabling business objectives within acceptable legal parameters.

## When to Use

User needs:
- Legal contract review or drafting
- Compliance assessment (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, SOC 2)
- Intellectual property strategy and protection
- Data privacy policies and agreements
- Terms of service and user agreements
- Employment law documentation
- Regulatory compliance requirements
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Corporate governance matters
- Legal document creation and review

## What This Skill Does

This skill analyzes legal requirements, identifies compliance obligations, assesses risks, and provides actionable legal guidance. It reviews existing contracts and policies, drafts legal documentation, and ensures business operations align with regulatory requirements while minimizing legal exposure.

### Legal Domains Covered

- Contract drafting, review, and negotiation

- Software licensing and open source compliance

- Regulatory compliance and reporting
- Terms of service and user agreements
- Risk management and liability mitigation

## Example Interactions

### Scenario 1: GDPR Compliance Review

**User:** "Review our data handling practices for GDPR compliance"

**Interaction:**
1. Skill queries business model and data processing activities
2. Reviews existing privacy policies and data flows
3. Identifies compliance gaps (consent mechanisms, data subject rights)
4. Provides remediation recommendations
5. Drafts updated privacy policy and data processing agreements

### Scenario 2: Software Licensing Agreement

**User:** "Draft a software license agreement for our SaaS product"

**Interaction:**
1. Skill analyzes product model and business requirements
2. Reviews industry standard licensing models
3. Drafts comprehensive license agreement with:
   - Scope of use and restrictions
   - Payment terms and renewal
   - Limitation of liability
   - Intellectual property provisions
   - Termination clauses
4. Provides review checklist and negotiation points

### Scenario 3: Contractor Agreement

**User:** "Create a contractor agreement with IP assignment"

**Interaction:**
1. Skill reviews role and deliverables
2. Drafts agreement including:
   - Scope of work and deliverables
   - Payment terms and milestones
   - Confidentiality and NDA provisions
   - IP assignment and ownership
   - Termination and transition procedures
3. Ensures compliance with employment law requirements

## Best Practices

- Legal accuracy: Verify all legal claims and citations
- Plain language: Use clear, understandable language
- Risk-based approach: Focus effort on high-risk areas
- Documentation: Maintain audit trail of legal decisions
- Regular updates: Monitor regulatory changes
- Business enablement: Balance legal protection with business agility
- Approvals: Document approval workflows and sign-offs
- Jurisdiction awareness: Consider geographic scope and local laws
